Overview

The first sharding-based parallel EVM PoS blockchain, bridging the gap between blockchain and AI, providing users with the ultimate tool in the cyberspace.

We can handle

#1 High TPS and Low Latency

Loadsharing Sharding

  • Each shard has its own shard chain Each block is evenly divided into chunks for different shards
  • Each block is evenly divided into chunks for different shards
  • Allow inter-shard communication
  • Scalable: higher speed by adding more shards

Parallel EVM

  • Parallel transaction assignments into different chunks
  • Dependency of transactions is appended as metadata to blocks
  • Parallel confirmations on each shard chain
  • Scalable: higher speed with more parallel confirmations

Fast Consensus Mechanism

  • fBFT consensus mechanism
  • BLS multi-signature scheme
  • Complexity grows linearly in the number of nodes (Complexity of BFT for Ethereum grows quadratically in the number of nodes)

#2 Low Cost, and Secure Transactions:

High Throughput

  • Our throughput can be scaled through the number of shards and the number of parallel EVM, lowering gas fee
  • Able to meet the high volume of demand of AI

Scaling through Layer 2 Blockchains

  • Our gas fee can be further reduced by deploying layer 2 ZK EVM blockchain; ZK EVM can provide AI data privacy
  • Edge computing facilitates GPU crowd sourcing for LLM
  • Storage network empowers AI data storage and data assetization

Security

  • The blockchain is secured by proof of stake (PoS)
  • The validators in each shard are randomly assigned using VDF+VRF
  • The validators will be reassigned each epoch, e.g., 20 hours
  • Limited voting power in each shard for a validator

#3 Decentralized AI

Layer 2 Edge Computing Network

  • Edge computing can reach billions of users
  • Empowering decentralized AI training, e.g., LLM
  • Crowd sourcing GPU for LLM for global participants
  • Small AI companies or even individuals can deploy AI algorithms on our network

Incentive Mechanism and Payment

  • Provide incentive mechanism for collabrative AI training
  • Comissionless usage of AI
  • Usage of AI can be facilitated by cryptocurrency and transactions on Zippy

Two-Sided Market for Data and AI services

  • Deploy our multimodal LLM and AIGC dapp, AI-based trading bot,
  • Two-sided marketplace for AI services and data generated by decentralized AI,e.g., NFT
  • Al algorithms and Data ownership can be minted by NFT
  • Data can be hosted in our decentralized storage network
